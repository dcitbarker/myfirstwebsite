Applications of Space technology

How we are making the earth a better place!

When the word space is mentioned,so many thoughts run through our minds.To some it means adventure. With space as our vantage, we are able to observe our planet globally.

The application of knowledge obtained in space technology goes a long way to help make the earth a better place. Some of the ways that space technology is used to improve life on earth includes the following

  • Use of satellites to track the Weather: Powerful sensors on weather satellites scan the earth, take measurements of reflected light and also infrared temperatures to give a clearer and precise picture of the weather.
  • Frontier technology for tackling climate change: Frontier technology is a new innovative and disruptive technology which helps to assess,mitigate and adapt to climate change. This method is very effective in controlling climate change.
  • The use of AI cameras to monitor wildlife crime: AI helps wildlife researchers in studying the wild animal species collectively and making strategies to protect them. It also tracks the pattern of wildlife and predicts the extinction of endangered animal species

    • In conclusion, The above listed reasons show how space technology can be applied to make the earth a better place. It also shows the great impact space technology has already had on the earth and the posibilities it could offer in the future.
