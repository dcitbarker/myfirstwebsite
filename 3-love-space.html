Why I Love The Idea of Space

- Beyond the cradle of humanity

When I was a kid, I loved to stare up at the sky in wonderment. I mean, it's huge and shiny and i used to imagine that there could be something above it.

I really got fascinated about the questions I go about asking and the answers I got. As I grew up i realized that studying space(what i meant as something above the sky) helps us understand our own world. It gives us better understanding of our planet and studying the other worlds of our solar system and beyond makes it clear that Earth is a precious oasis of life.

Since i am very passionate about going to space in future, I am driven by this saying of Konstantin Tsiolkovsky:

"The Earth is the cradle of humanity, but mankind cannot stay in the cradle forever"
